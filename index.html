What Is Climate Change?

Climate change is the long-term alteration of temperature and typical weather patterns in a place, including global warming caused by man-made greenhouse gas emissions, with results such as large-scale shifts in weather patterns. Climate change causes frequent and intense heat waves, storms, droughts, rising sea levels, melting glaciers, and warming oceans.

How Has It Affected Us?

Climate change has already impacted our lives by rising sea levels and global warming, however it might not be too late to start a new revolution of getting rid of single use plastics and instead using biodegradable ones.

What Can Climate Change Do?

Climate change causes frequent and intense heat waves, storms, droughts, rising sea levels, melting glaciers, and warming oceans. These natural disasters destroy animal habitats and wreak havoc on people's livelihoods and communities.
